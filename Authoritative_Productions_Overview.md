# Authoritative Productions

## Company Overview

Authoritative Productions is an AI creative studio based in Singapore, founded and led by Jaron. The business represents a strategic merger of traditional production capabilities with cutting-edge AI services under one unified brand. With over 8 years of established production experience, the company is uniquely positioned at the intersection of traditional content production and emerging AI-powered creative services.

## Three Core Pillars

1. **AI UGC / Avatars** — AI-generated user-generated content and digital avatar creation
2. **Generative AI Image & Video Production** — End-to-end AI-powered visual content creation
3. **AI Staging Services** — Virtual staging solutions (particularly relevant for real estate applications)

## Revenue Strategy

The business operates on a strategic **60/40 revenue split**:

- **60% Client Services** — Project-based work for agencies, brands, and corporate clients
- **40% Virtual Influencer IP** — Building proprietary virtual influencer properties as recurring revenue assets
