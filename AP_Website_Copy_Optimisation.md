# Strategic Overview

Before diving into line-by-line changes, here are the overarching principles guiding this optimisation:

### 1. Lead with outcomes, not descriptions
The current site tells visitors what you do. The optimised copy shows them what they get. Every service description should answer the prospect's unspoken question: "Why should I care?"

### 2. Sharpen the hybrid positioning
Your biggest competitive moat is the fusion of 8+ years of traditional production credibility with AI capabilities. The current copy mentions this but doesn't hammer it home. The optimised version makes it the throughline of every section.

### 3. Add commercial urgency
The current copy reads as a portfolio. It needs to read as a sales tool. Small tweaks â€” specificity in outcomes, social proof phrasing, and a stronger CTA â€” shift the site from "informational" to "conversion-oriented."

### 4. Tighten the language
Several sections use filler phrases or repeat the same value prop in different words. The optimised copy is leaner, punchier, and respects the visitor's scroll speed.
